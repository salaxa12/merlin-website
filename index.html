About Us

About

Through helping organizations acquire and procure goods and services, we lead with a commitment to quality, safety, and integrity across our teams.

Merlin LLC – FZ is a UAE based procurement and delivery management firm that offers a wide range of end-to-end supply chain management solutions to clients around the world. Merlin utilizes latest technology, its proven methodology, and purchasing specialists from all around the world bringing together over 20 years of industry experience to deliver highly efficient, best value, sustainable procurement, and responsible sourcing services.

Objective

Strategically located in the dynamic city of Dubai, United Arab Emirates which serves as a nexus for the East and West, Merlin’s objective is to become region’s leading procurement firm anchored in serving humanitarian and non-commercial industry.

Mission

Merlin's mission is to work closely with customers on every supply project and to ensure the goods and services required by clients are delivered on time without compromising on quality.
